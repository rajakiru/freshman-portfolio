Research

Effect of Emotion Synchronization for Robot Facial Expressions

With the recent developments in fields like Artificial Intelligence and Robotics, the rise of social robots is not far away. For social robots to establish better human interaction, they must be able to interact with people casually. In this research, we introduce the idea of empathy in robots by syncing our emotions with the robot's facial expression.
